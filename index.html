Delete Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users of SPACE BATTLE – SHOOTING GAME can request the deletion of their account and personal data.

1. How to Request Account & Data Deletion

Users can request deletion inside the game by:

  • Entering their registered email address
  • Agreeing to the terms
  • Tapping the DELETE ACCOUNT button

Users may also send a deletion request to the support email.

Please include:

  • Name
  • Registered email address
  • User ID (if available)

2. Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • In-game progress (levels, coins, statistics)
  • Gameplay activity history
  • Support communication history

Some anonymized analytics or crash log data may be temporarily retained for security, diagnostics, or legal compliance. This retained data is not linked to any user identity.

3. Deletion Timeframe

After receiving a deletion request, applicable data will be deleted within 1 minute.

4. Can a Deletion Request Be Cancelled?

No. Once the deletion process begins, it cannot be undone.

5. After Deletion

  • All progress and rewards are permanently erased
  • The account cannot be restored
  • A new account must be created to play again
