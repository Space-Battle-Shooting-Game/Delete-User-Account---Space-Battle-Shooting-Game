Request Account Deletion

This page allows users of Your App Name to request deletion of their account and associated data. This applies to accounts created using an email + password inside the app.

How to Delete Your Account From Inside the App

You can delete your account directly in the app by following these steps:

  1. Open Your App Name
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision

This will permanently remove your account data from our Firebase database.

If You Cannot Access the App

If you are unable to open the app (for example: lost device, error, or uninstall), you may request account deletion by email:

Email: support@yourdomain.com

Please include the email address used for your account. We will delete your account within 7 days of receiving the request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address
  • Your password stored in Firebase
  • Your display name / profile name
  • Your gameplay progress (if stored under your user account)
  • Your cloud save data
  • Your authentication-related reset tokens
  • Any other data stored under your user entry in our Firebase database: /users/<your-sanitized-email>

Data We Retain

We do not retain any personal information after account deletion. All related data is permanently erased.

Retention Period

Your data will be deleted within 7 days of submitting a deletion request.
