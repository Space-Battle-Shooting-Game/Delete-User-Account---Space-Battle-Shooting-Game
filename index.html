Delete Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users of SPACE BATTLE - SHOOTING GAME can request the deletion of their account and personal data, as required by Google Play's Data Deletion policy.

How to Request Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion, Provide an registered email in the Game App:

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Your name
  • Your registered email address
  • Your User ID (if available in the app)

Check "Agree to Terms and Conditions" and Press "DELETE ACCOUNT" Button.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once we receive your deletion request, we will permanently delete the following:

  • Your Name
  • Your Email Address
  • Your Payment Details (Easypaisa, JazzCash, Bank Account Number)
  • Your In-Game Activity (coins earned, levels played)
  • Your transaction/withdrawal history*

*Some financial records may be securely retained if required for legal compliance (e.g., tax or anti-fraud regulations), but will no longer be linked to your identity.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

After receiving your deletion request:

  • Your account and personal information will be deleted within 1 Minute.

Can I Cancel My Deletion Request?

No. If the deletion process has been send, your data will be deleted under 1 Minute.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • You will no longer be able to access your account.
  • Your progress, coins, and game activity will be permanently removed.
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to play again.
